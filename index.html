Privacy Policy for SnapAnswer

Effective Date: August 24, 2025

SnapAnswer values your privacy. This policy explains what information our macOS app collects, how it is used, and your rights as a user.

Information We Collect

  • Screenshots & Text Recognition: Screenshots you capture are processed locally on your Mac to extract text using Apple’s Vision framework (OCR). SnapAnswer does not upload your screenshots to our servers.
  • AI Requests: When you request an answer, the recognized text is sent securely to OpenAI’s API to generate a response.
  • API Keys: If you add your own OpenAI API key, it is stored securely in your Mac’s Keychain and is never shared with us.

How We Use Information

  • To perform OCR locally on your device.
  • To send extracted text to OpenAI’s API in order to provide answers.
  • We do not use your data for analytics, tracking, or advertising.

Data Sharing

  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.
  • The only third party involved in processing is OpenAI, which receives the text you send when requesting answers.

Your Rights & Choices

  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may delete your OpenAI API key from Settings at any time.
  • No data is stored by us outside your device.

Security

We rely on macOS Keychain to store your API key securely and use HTTPS when communicating with OpenAI’s API.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
